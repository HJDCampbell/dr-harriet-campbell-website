About Me

Grounded in science,
guided by compassion.

I'm Dr Harriet Campbell, a HCPC-registered Counselling Psychologist with extensive experience supporting individuals through anxiety, depression, relationship difficulties, grief, trauma, and life transitions.

My practice is founded on the belief that every person holds the capacity for growth and healing. I bring both clinical expertise and genuine warmth to each session, creating a space where you feel truly heard.

I offer online sessions, meaning you can access support from the comfort and safety of your own home, wherever you are in the UK.

My Approach

How I work with you

I draw on a range of evidence-based therapeutic approaches, carefully tailored to each individual. No two people are the same — and neither are their paths to wellbeing.

🌱

Person-Centred

You are the expert on your own life. I provide unconditional positive regard and a non-judgemental space for you to explore what matters most.

🧠

CBT & ACT

Cognitive Behavioural and Acceptance & Commitment approaches help you understand thought patterns and develop practical strategies for lasting change.

🤝

Relational

The therapeutic relationship itself is a powerful vehicle for healing. I place great importance on building a genuine, trusting connection with you.

Sessions

What to expect

All sessions take place online via a secure, confidential video platform — accessible from anywhere in the UK, at a time that works for you.

We'll begin with an initial consultation to understand your needs, discuss what you're hoping to achieve, and see if we're the right fit for each other. There's no pressure or commitment required at this stage.

  • Free 20-minute introductory call
  • 50-minute individual sessions
  • Flexible scheduling, including evenings
  • Short-term and open-ended therapy available
  • Strict confidentiality & HCPC ethical standards
